Our Services

Whatever the scale of your vision, one team brings four decades of craftsmanship to every project.

01 — Turn-Key Construction

Fully Custom Homes

From a napkin sketch or full blueprints, we manage the entire build — design, permitting, foundation, framing, finishes, and final walkthrough — so you can simply turn the key and move in.

  • Custom & semi-custom homes
  • Log & timber-frame homes
  • Land sourcing & lot guidance
  • Full project management
02 — Additions

More Room to Live

Growing family or just need more space? We design and build additions that look like they were always part of the home — matching rooflines, finishes, and character.

  • Room & second-story additions
  • In-law & bonus suites
  • Sunrooms & finished basements
  • Garages & outbuildings
03 — Renovations

Transform Your Space

Kitchens, baths, and whole-home remodels that blend modern living with lasting craftsmanship. We handle the details that make a renovation feel effortless.

  • Kitchen & bath remodels
  • Whole-home renovations
  • Historic & farmhouse updates
  • Custom millwork & built-ins
04 — Building Repairs

Honest, Lasting Repairs

From structural concerns to the small details that protect your investment, we diagnose the real problem and fix it to last.

  • Structural & foundation repair
  • Rot, water & storm damage
  • Decks, porches & railings
  • Roofing & siding repair
05 — Painting & Exterior

Finishes Built to Last

Interior and exterior finishes that look beautiful on day one and hold up to Virginia's seasons for years. Color consultation included.

  • Interior & exterior painting
  • Siding, trim & soffit
  • Pressure washing & prep
  • Staining & sealing
06 — Excavation

The Right Start

Every great build begins below grade. Our site preparation, grading, and groundwork set a solid, level foundation for whatever comes next.

  • Site clearing & prep
  • Grading & drainage
  • Foundation excavation
  • Driveways & utilities
