Our Story

A name built on four decades of craftsmanship.

Since 1982, Ronald M. Hawes Inc. has been a trusted custom home builder in Loudoun County. We're locals — which means when your project begins with finding the right piece of land, we can guide you there through relationships built over a lifetime in this community.

Whether you arrive with full blueprints, a sketch on a napkin, or simply an idea, we bring the experience and integrity to see it through — from the first conversation to the day you turn the key.
